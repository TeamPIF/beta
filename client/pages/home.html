ABOUT

Welcome to Food for Change! This organization is dedicated to feeding the hungry and changing the perception towards those in need. Local Businesses have teamed up with Food for Change so that anyone in need can step in and grab a meal. We can’t do this without you!

HOW IT WORKS

  1. Local Businesses register with Food for Change.

  2. Community members can donate a meal using a Food for Change tablet at checkout or can donate remotely on this site.

  3. Those in need no longer need to panhandle and can walk in to affiliated locations and receive a meal.

STORIES

I used to spend hours sitting outside shops feeling ashamed for having to beg for money to eat. Now I can walk into a shop and get a meal while I spend my time finding ways to provide for myself. I’m so grateful for this organization and one day I want to be the one donating. We’re all human and we should care for one another.
- Bill

Many people in Ithaca feel similarly to Bill. Begging for basic human rights can make a person in need feel shameful and isolated. In reality, those that need help are contributing members of society and being kind to those in need will both motivate them to seek sustainable livelihoods and others to engage in acts of kindness.
